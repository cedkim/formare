#### Overview

`u-{Property}-{size}-{breakpoint}`

- `Margin` to margin around the element.
- `Padding` to apply padding around the element.
- `MarginLeft` to apply only left margin or padding.
- `MarginRight` to apply only right margin or padding.
- `MarginTop` to apply only top margin or padding.
- `MarginBottom` to apply only bottom margin or padding.
- `MarginHorizontal` to apply both left and right margin or padding.
- `MarginVertical` to apply both top and bottom margin or padding.

#### Sizes

Ad proident labore laborum labore ea mollit exercitation nostrud in.

- None: Applies base margin or padding (`1rem`)
- `small`: Applies small margin or padding (`0.667rem`)
- `xsmall`: Applies extra small margin or padding (`0.334rem`)
- `2xsmall`: Applies ultra small margin or padding (`0.223rem`)
- `large`: Applies large margin or padding (`1.5rem`)
- `xlarge`: Applies extra large margin or padding (`3rem`)
- `2xlarge`: Applies ultra large margin or padding (`4.5rem`)
